AI Hackathon: Civic Innovation Challenge
On behalf of our team and our Module Head, Ibrahim Naeem, we welcome you all to the AI Hackathon Module!
We are thrilled to have 22 teams bringing their energy, creativity, and technical skills to the table today! Our mission? Turn civic complaints into powerful AI solutions that improve everyday life .

--- SLIDE ---
What on Earth is AI?
AI is practical digital superpower : smart code that recognises patterns, automates tasks, and helps us solve complex civic problems faster. Think prediction, classification, automation and accessible interfaces not sci‑fi.

--- SLIDE ---
What Counts as a Civic Problem?
Any daily issue that harms communities or public services. Examples:
Poor traffic flow and road safety
Poor waste collection and pollution
Limited access to primary healthcare
Unreliable electricity and load-shedding
Education gaps and youth unemployment

--- SLIDE ---
Challenge Brief — Build for Pakistan
Design an AI-powered solution targeting a real civic problem in Pakistan. The five inspiration tracks are starting points—feel free to combine or go beyond them.
Traffic & Road Safety
Predict congestion, detect violations, optimise routing, or improve pedestrian safety.
Waste & Environment
Optimise collection routes, detect illegal dumping, or map pollution hotspots.
Education & Employment
Skills matching, micro‑learning pathways, or job-placement nudges for youth.
Healthcare Access
Tele-triage, clinic mapping, appointment matching, or medical supply forecasting.
Energy & Power
Forecast load, suggest local scheduling, or detect outages faster.

--- SLIDE ---
Allowed Deliverables — Build Whatever Solves It
Options include an AI model, website, mobile app, extension, dashboard, or any hybrid prototype. The only rule: original work—no copying or reskinning existing products.
Aim for a minimal viable demo that clearly shows AI's role.

--- SLIDE ---
Ground Rules & Logistics
3-Hour Window
Setup, build, and final demo all within the sprint. Respect the clock.
Team Presence
All members must be physically present for the duration.
Original Work
No direct cloning. Reuse libraries and APIs, but produce new integrations and designs.
AI Welcome
Use models and tools, but document sources and ensure responsible usage.

--- SLIDE ---
Judging: How to Win (100 Points)
Pitch must cover:
Problem definition and impact
How AI is used (models, data, UX)
Working demo or clear prototype
Score breakdown:
Creativity & Uniqueness — 45
Real impact for Pakistan — 35
Working demo — 20.

--- SLIDE ---
Pitch Tips — Make the Demo Sing
Start with a human story
One clear example of who is helped and why it matters.
Show AI, simply
Explain model function in one sentence and show inputs → outputs.
Demo core feature
Even a partially working flow is better than many slides.
Plan a 2‑minute closing
Summarise impact, next steps, and what help you need to continue.
